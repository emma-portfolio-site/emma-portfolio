Who is Emma

Emma Rainville is the Co-Founder of Shockwave Solutions, LLC. She is an accomplished Executive Integrator / Integrator Coach available for engagement. Emma brings a broad range of leadership experience, including operations, logistics, manufacturing, fulfillment, strategic planning, and P&L management with over 20 years of experience.

Emma is a results-oriented, decisive, and direct executive with proven success in leading organizations. Emma does not give advice but executes the strategies to get the company moving in the direction of the vision.

Emma has led 8 VTO creations, sat in over 48 Company Quarterly meetings, 11 Annual meetings, and she has led hundreds of L-10s. She is a proud member and graduate of Mark Winter's EOS Integrator™ Academy. Emma has worked across various industries, company sizes, and funding stages. Emma has also mentored and coached numerous Integrators and Visionaries, helping them get to the next level and achieve their goals.

How I Work

An Integrator is the person who is the tie-breaker for the Leadership Team, is the glue for the organization, holds everything together, sets the pace,, is accountable for the P&L results, executes the business plan, holds the Leadership Team accountable, and is the steady force in the organization.

The Integrator also creates organizational clarity, communication, and consistency; operates more on logic; drives results; forces resolution, focus, team unity, prioritization and follow-through; is the filter for all of the Visionary’s ideas; harmoniously integrates the Leadership Team; and helps to remove obstacles and barriers.

So how can an integrator change your organization?

Establishing Effective Meetings

Meetings should have a purpose and benefit everyone in attendance. Many companies don’t even get close to this ideal. I work to introduce effective, consistent structures for meetings, removing a common source of frustration.

Working Together

Everyone in a company should be on the same page - everyone should understand exactly what they’re working towards, and what their individual role in achieving that goal is. I work to get everyone working on the same level, improving efficiency, results, and employee satisfaction.

Improving Customer Satisfaction

Customer satisfaction is vastly more important than you may think. Giving your customers great service doesn’t just make them happy - it should cut refunds and chargebacks, prevent legal liability, and more. I work to take customer service to a new level.

Launching New Products

Planning to launch a new product? I have led clients through countless product launches. I handle every part of the journey, from copyright searches and product planning to achieving excellent production quality at a low cost.

Developing Profitability

Profitability isn’t a set figure. It’s something that can be improved. By getting you better deals for manufacturing, fulfillment, and more, cutting costs while introducing new revenue sources, I make sure that you keep more of the money you make.

Tracking and Improving CLTV

Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) is one of my single biggest focuses. In short, it tracks how much a customer spends across their entire engagement with you. I work to establish and improve CLTV, making each customer worth more to you.
